OUR STORY

Bridging Skills & Opportunities

Founded in 2024 in Hyderabad, WorkOra India was born from a simple observation: despite India's massive workforce, skilled blue-collar workers struggled to find reliable employment while employers had difficulty finding quality talent.

Today, we're proud to have facilitated over 10K job connections across 28 states, improving lives and supporting India's growing economy.

Mission & Values

Empowering India's blue-collar workforce with dignity and opportunity

Our Mission

To empower India's blue-collar workforce by creating accessible pathways to dignified employment and fair wages, while helping businesses find skilled, reliable workers to fuel their growth.

Our Vision

A future where every skilled worker in India has access to stable, well-paying jobs with dignity, safety, and growth opportunities, contributing to a more equitable society.

Our Values

We operate with integrity, transparency, and respect for all workers. We believe in inclusivity, fairness, and creating opportunities that uplift communities across urban and rural India.
